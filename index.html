Time Tracking Me

Find where your day went

It is end of the day. You feel exhausted.
You feel like you have been working all day long but somehow you did not finish things you planned to.

Feels familiar?

Wouldn't be great to know where you day went? How much time do you spend on daily routines, and how much did it actually go on your little secret project?

We are trying to develop product which can help you with that. Application that makes it easy to track your time. Not just your work but all your activities. New player in productivity arena.
You can checkout functionalities below and participate by commenting, sharing the idea and donating the project

Track

Start tracking your time

Just type in activity you are currently doing.

Use predictive autocomplete if you have tracked activity before

You can enter your regular and planed activities for quick start tracking. All you need is to click or tap you screen to start tracking

Delete activities you do not want in your statistics

Projects

Group your activities into projects if you want total time spent per type

Get total time for your projects and use information to bill your customer

Check total time you are spending on fitness, commuting or going out with your friends

Stats

Get your statistics and summary results

Filter by projects and dates

Group by tasks or by activities

Export your data in CSV so it could be used anywhere

Plan

Plan your activities

Add your usual activities so you can quick start tracking them from main screen

Plan in advance and schedule your activities. Add activation date to your activity and activity will become visible on quick start from that date onwards
